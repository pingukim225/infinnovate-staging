Our Programs

Three Pathways to
Real-World Readiness

Infinnovate's curriculum is built around three interconnected pillars — each designed to give students skills they'll use for the rest of their lives.

💼

Business & Entrepreneurship

Students don't just learn about business — they build one. From identifying a market opportunity to creating financial projections and pitching to real mentors, this pathway mirrors the startup experience.

By the end, every student walks away with a portfolio-ready business plan, presentation skills, and the confidence to think like a founder.

  • Market research & competitive analysis
  • Business model canvas & lean startup methodology
  • Brand identity, marketing strategy, and positioning
  • Financial projections & unit economics
  • Investor pitch preparation & live presentation
  • Team building & leadership dynamics

Financial Literacy

Most adults wish someone had taught them about money in school. We don't wait for "most adults" — we start now. Students learn the fundamentals of personal finance, investing, and wealth-building before they graduate high school.

This isn't theoretical. Students open simulated portfolios, track real market data, and make investment decisions with guided mentorship.

  • Personal budgeting & savings strategies
  • Credit scores, loans, and debt management
  • Stock market simulation & portfolio management
  • Compound interest & time value of money
  • Tax fundamentals every young adult needs
  • Retirement planning & long-term wealth building

AI & Technology

The next generation won't just use AI — they'll build with it. Our technology pathway teaches students how artificial intelligence works, how to leverage it responsibly, and how to create solutions with modern digital tools.

Students complete hands-on projects that demonstrate real skills — from building AI-powered apps to analyzing data sets and automating workflows.

Students take their skills beyond the classroom — interviewing local businesses, identifying real-world inefficiencies, and applying AI tools to solve them. Real companies. Real problems. Real solutions.

  • Introduction to AI, machine learning, and automation
  • Prompt engineering & working with AI assistants
  • Data analysis & visualization fundamentals
  • No-code app building & prototyping
  • Digital marketing & social media strategy
  • Ethics in AI & responsible technology use

Our Framework

The ESBIE Model

Inspired by Robert Kiyosaki's Cashflow Quadrant, the ESBIE model maps the journey from employee to entrepreneur — and we guide students through every stage.

👤
Employee
Learn the fundamentals: discipline, teamwork, and professional skills
🚀
Start-Up
Ideate, validate, and launch your own venture from scratch
🏢
Big Business
Scale operations, build teams, and create systems that grow
📈
Investor
Make money work for you through smart investing strategies
⚡
Entrepreneur
Create lasting impact by solving real problems at scale
