California Compliance

AB 2927 & Financial Literacy

California's AB 2927 mandates that all high school students receive financial literacy education. Schools are navigating new requirements to integrate personal finance into their curriculum — and many lack the resources or expertise to do it effectively.

Infinnovate provides a ready-made solution. Our financial literacy curriculum covers every required topic area — budgeting, credit, investing, taxes, and more — delivered by instructors with decades of real-world finance experience.

We work directly with your administration to ensure our programs meet compliance requirements while delivering genuine educational value to students.
