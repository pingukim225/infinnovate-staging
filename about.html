About Infinnovate

Built to bridge the life readiness gap.

Infinnovate helps students move beyond classroom credentials into practical confidence — through business literacy, financial literacy, and AI applications anchored in purpose and leadership.

Our mission

Drive value around the world — one purpose, one idea, one entrepreneur at a time.

Founded in 2021, Infinnovate exists to inspire infinite potential and equip students with the confidence to prosper, innovate, and lead. The model is experiential, local, and intentionally future-facing.
