What We Teach

Three Pathways to Real-World Readiness

Infinnovate equips students with financial literacy, business leadership, and AI skills through an immersive, hands-on academy.

💼

Business & Entrepreneurship

Students build real business plans, pitch to mentors, and learn the mechanics of starting and scaling a venture — from ideation to revenue.

Covers market research, competitive analysis, branding, financial projections, and investor presentations. Students graduate with a portfolio-ready business plan.

Explore Pathway →
📊

Financial Literacy

From budgeting basics to investment strategy, students learn the money skills that schools skip — preparing them for financial independence before college.

Includes personal finance, credit management, stock market simulation, compound interest, tax fundamentals, and retirement planning concepts.

Explore Pathway →
🤖

AI & Technology

Students explore artificial intelligence, automation, and digital tools — learning to build with technology, not just consume it.

Hands-on projects with AI tools, prompt engineering, data analysis, no-code app building, and understanding how technology reshapes industries.

Explore Pathway →
