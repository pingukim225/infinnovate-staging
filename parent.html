Inside the academy

A mini-economy where learning is real.

🏦

Mini-Economy Ecosystem

Students earn, spend, and invest inside a functioning classroom economy - complete with student-run businesses, banking roles, and marketplace events. It mirrors the real world so the habits stick.

📋

Obeya Room - Transparent Progress

Borrowed from Toyota's management system, our Obeya Room gives parents and students a visible dashboard of goals, milestones, and growth. No guessing - you can see the progress on the wall.

Signature events
