Our Programs

Built for real outcomes.

Every Infinnovate program is experiential, practical, and designed around the ESBIE framework — guiding students from their first role as an Employee all the way to becoming an Entrepreneur.

01

Infinnovate for Education

District-aligned curriculum delivered in schools. Business literacy, financial literacy, and AI fluency woven into the academic calendar.

02

Academy Programs

After-school and weekend intensives at the Infinnovate Innovation Center. Project-based, simulation-driven, leadership-focused.

03

Events & Competitions

Live events, student storefronts, business challenges, and entrepreneurship labs. Confidence comes from experience.

Your Journey

One Academy.
Five Personas.
Infinnovate connects all five.

Infinnovate, Inc. was founded to bridge the gap between traditional education and the real world. Through our flagship program, Infinnovate for Education, we provide interactive, impact-driven learning that develops real-world experience in business leadership, financial literacy, and responsible AI.

E

The Employee Experience

Responsibility, teamwork, communication, and contribution inside a real organization.

S

The Small Business

Operations, customer service, problem-solving, and the reality of running a small business.

B

The Big Business

Strategy, systems thinking, corporate leadership, and how large organizations operate.

I

The Investor

Capital allocation, risk assessment, portfolio thinking, and investing in the future.

E

The Entrepreneur

Leadership, initiative, resilience, and execution — create, launch, and lead.
