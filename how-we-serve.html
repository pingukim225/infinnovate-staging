Programs & Pathways

How We Serve Students, Families, and Schools

Infinnovate provides experiential education programs designed to build real-world capability, confidence, and future-ready skills. Our model combines academy programs, district partnerships, innovation labs, and real-world simulations to help students develop the mindset needed for success in the modern world.

Our Programs

Built for Real-World Outcomes

Every Infinnovate program is designed around experience, responsibility, and real application. Our model follows the ESBIE framework — guiding students from their first experience as an Employee, to Small Business builder, to Big Business leader, to Investor, and ultimately Entrepreneur. Students don't just learn concepts. They practice decision-making, leadership, financial thinking, and innovation through structured, real-world experiences.

01

Infinnovate for Education

District-integrated curriculum designed for schools and expanded learning programs. Students build business, financial, leadership, and AI literacy through interactive, real-world projects aligned with academic development.

02

Academy Programs

After-school, weekend, and summer programs delivered through the Infinnovate Innovation Center and partner locations. Project-based learning, simulations, leadership training, entrepreneurship, and AI fluency are integrated into every experience.

03

Events, Competitions & Challenges

Student storefronts, innovation challenges, business competitions, and live events that allow students to apply what they learn in real situations. Confidence comes from experience.

Learning Pathways

From 4th Grade Through High School

Infinnovate serves students from elementary through high school with a structured pathway that builds capability year by year. Each stage develops independence, confidence, leadership, and real-world readiness — preparing students for advanced academics, careers, and entrepreneurship.

4–5

Grades 4–5

Foundation

Introduction to financial literacy, teamwork, communication, and problem-solving. Students begin to understand how money, business, and responsibility work in the real world.

6–7

Grades 6–7

Exploration

Public speaking, project-based learning, small business simulations, and structured challenges. Students learn how ideas become real projects.

8–9

Grades 8–9

Build

Leadership roles, advanced projects, innovation labs, and entrepreneurship experiences. Students begin managing real responsibilities and making real decisions.

10–12

Grades 10–12

Lead

Mentorship, internships, advanced strategy, investment thinking, and career preparation. Students develop the mindset needed for college, career, and entrepreneurship.

The Infinnovate Model

One Academy. Five Personas. One Complete System.

Infinnovate was built to close the gap between traditional education and the real world. Through the ESBIE framework, students experience the roles that define modern success: Employee → Small Business → Big Business → Investor → Entrepreneur. By stepping into each role, students learn how the world works — not just how school works. This system develops confidence, financial intelligence, leadership, and real-world awareness.

E

Employee

Responsibility, teamwork, communication, and understanding how organizations operate. Students learn how to contribute, collaborate, and perform.
