Our Programs

Built for real outcomes.

Every Infinnovate program is experiential, practical, and designed around the ESBIE framework — guiding students from their first role as an Employee all the way to becoming an Entrepreneur.

01

Infinnovate for Education

District-aligned curriculum delivered in schools. Business literacy, financial literacy, and AI fluency woven into the academic calendar.

02

Academy Programs

After-school and weekend intensives at the Infinnovate Innovation Center. Project-based, simulation-driven, leadership-focused.

03

Events & Competitions

Live events, student storefronts, business challenges, and entrepreneurship labs. Confidence comes from experience.

Learning Pathways

4th Grade Through High School

Our programs serve students from 4th grade through high school, with age-appropriate curriculum at every level. Each stage builds on the last — developing capability, confidence, and real-world readiness.

4–5

Grades 4–5

Foundation

Introduction to financial literacy, teamwork, and creative problem-solving. Students learn through games, group challenges, and basic business concepts.

6–7

Grades 6–7

Explorer

Deeper business simulations, public speaking practice, and first exposure to AI tools. Students build their own student businesses and present ideas.

8–9

Grades 8–9

Builder

Advanced ESBIE framework, leadership roles, team management, and real-world projects. Students compete in entrepreneurship challenges and manage budgets.

10–12

Grades 10–12

Leader

Mentorship, investment thinking, advanced AI fluency, and capstone projects. Students mentor younger cohorts and prepare for college and career readiness.
